The Ref Called WHAT?!

Offensive Holding

NCAA Football Fouls

Rulebook Definition

The hand(s) and arm(s) shall not be used to grasp, pull, hook, clamp or encircle in any way that illegally impedes or illegally obstructs an opponent.

Penalty Assessed

Fouls that occur behind the neutral zone: 10 yards from previous spot

Fouls that occur behind the Offensive team's goal line: Safety

Similar Calls
